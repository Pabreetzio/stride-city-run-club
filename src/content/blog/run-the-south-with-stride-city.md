Stride City Run Club is putting together a team for **Run the South Half Marathon, 10K & 5K**, one of Charlotte's summer race events at **McAlpine Creek Park**. Known as "The Hottest Half," the race includes multiple distance options across a two-day format, including a Friday evening 5K and Saturday morning 10K and Half Marathon.

The event takes place at **McAlpine Creek Park, 8711 Monroe Rd, Charlotte, NC 28212**. Current event details list the **5K for Friday, June 19, 2026 at 6:15 PM**, followed by the **Half Marathon on Saturday, June 20, 2026 at 7:00 AM** and the **10K on Saturday, June 20, 2026 at 7:30 AM**.

Run the South is a great fit for Stride City because the course is described as fast and flat, with a route through mostly shaded greenways and wide open gravel paths. Whether you are chasing a PR, trying a new distance, or just looking for a fun race-day atmosphere with familiar faces, this is a strong summer goal race for the club.

All distances include race swag such as running sunglasses, a shirt option, and a custom finisher medal. The race also offers virtual options for runners who want to participate on their own schedule.

## Join the Stride City team

Stride City Run Club will have a team at Run the South, and we would love for you to join us. Register for the distance that works best for you, then look for the Stride City Run Club team option when signing up.

## Race options

- **5K** — Friday, June 19 at 6:15 PM
- **10K** — Saturday, June 20 at 7:30 AM
- **Half Marathon** — Saturday, June 20 at 7:00 AM
- **Challenge options** — Run the Friday 5K, then come back Saturday for the 10K or Half Marathon

## What to expect from Stride City

As race day gets closer, we will share meetup details, team timing, and any packet pickup or pre-race plans. Wear your Stride City gear if you have it, bring water, and plan for summer heat. All paces are welcome. Come to race, run, walk, cheer, or support the team.

Run the South is a chance to show up together, celebrate the progress we have made on Wednesday nights, and bring that same Stride City energy to the starting line.
